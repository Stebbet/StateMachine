About
This application is a tool for designing and simulating finite-state machines. Including discrete finite automata (DFA) as well as Mealy and Moore machines. It is aimed at A-Level to Undergraduate Computer Science and Engineering students who wish to learn about, refresh their knowledge, or simply design and work with finite-state machines. This site is continuously in development so if you have found a bug or want to offer some feedback don't hesitate to contact me (see below) and let me know!

Open Source!

This application is completely open-source, access it on my GitHub page on the right. Feel free to fork it and make a pull request, or develop your own application off the back of this one!.
